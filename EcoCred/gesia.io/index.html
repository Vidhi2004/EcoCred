    Green Credits

    Green credits, particularly in the context of carbon credit marketplaces, play a crucial role in incentivizing and rewarding companies for reducing their carbon emissions and adopting sustainable practices. our Ecocred act as a pivotal platform in this ecosystem, facilitating the trading and management of carbon credits.

    GESIA platform announced on April 22, 2024, that it has completed the development of a new mainnet to support the voluntary carbon credit market and invest in projects that support the NET-ZERO goal in response to the global climate crisis.
    GESIA new mainnet enhances the reliability and efficiency of carbon credit data while offering smart contracts for carbon emission calculations. This allows other services and payment apps to participate and enables individuals to directly erase their carbon footprints. The entire process from the creation to the retirement of carbon credits can be transparently monitored, integrating functionalities that facilitate this transparency. The mainnet is based on a Three-Tier Merkle chain structure divided into three sections: carbon emission, offset, and neutrality. This structure significantly reduces the problems of 'Double Counting' and 'Double Dipping' in the voluntary carbon credit market. It also supports STO (Security Token Offering) technology using digital spot securities, making it compatible with the SEC-approved spot Bitcoin ETF market in the USA and the token security market in Korea.
    GESIA innovative CarbonMonster app understands the significance of the voluntary carbon credit market and supports members by allowing them to purchase and donate carbon credits using corporate or card points. This enables members to reinvest in carbon projects directly or indirectly and contribute to the ESG management of companies.
